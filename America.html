AMERICA: THE LAND OF CONTRAST

America includes North, Central and South America. It is a vast and diverse continent, rich in history, culture and geography. From the icy arctic tundra to the Amazon rainforest, and from ancient civilizations to modern cities, the Americas tell a story of migration, resistance, innovation and transformation.

The Americas stretch from the Arctic Circle in the north to Cape Horn in the south, encompassing every climate zone on Earth. This landmass has been home to great civilizations like the Maya, Aztec, and Inca, and today hosts some of the world's most dynamic economies and cultures.
