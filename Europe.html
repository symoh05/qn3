EUROPE: THE HEART OF WESTERN CIVILIZATION

Europe is one of the 7 continents of the world, known for its rich culture, diverse countries and long histories. It's home to over 700 million people and includes some of the world's most influential cities like Paris, Rome, and Berlin.

From the ancient ruins of Greece to the medieval castles of Central Europe, from the Renaissance art of Italy to the modern design of Scandinavia, Europe offers an unparalleled journey through human history and achievement.
