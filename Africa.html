AFRICA: THE SPIRIT OF AFRICAN HERITAGE

Africa, the cradle of humanity, is a continent rich in diversity, resilience, wildlife and tradition. From its majestic landscapes to its vibrant cultures, Africa tells stories that span millennia of kingdoms, migrations, innovation and unity in diversity.

With 54 sovereign nations, Africa offers an unparalleled variety of ecosystems, from the Sahara desert to lush rainforests, and from savannah grasslands to stunning coastlines.
