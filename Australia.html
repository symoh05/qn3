AUSTRALIA & NEW ZEALAND: VOICES OF THE SOUTHERN LAND

Australia and New Zealand, lands of ancient wisdom and breathtaking natural beauty, hold deep cultural roots shaped by the First Peoples (Aboriginal Australians and Māori). These island nations, though remote, are rich in tradition, storytelling and resilience. Their histories reflect both the enduring strengths of indigenous cultures and profound transformations brought by colonization and modern migration.

From the red deserts of Australia's Outback to New Zealand's majestic fjords, this region offers some of the world's most spectacular landscapes and unique wildlife found nowhere else on Earth.
